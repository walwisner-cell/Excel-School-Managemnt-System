Academics

A full Liberian curriculum, Nursery through Grade 12

We follow Liberia's 6-3-3 education structure, giving every student a clear path from early childhood through senior secondary school.

Grade Levels

Our Academic Structure

🧸

Early Childhood

Nursery, K1, and K2 — building the foundation for reading, numeracy, and social development.

📘

Primary School

Grades 1 through 6 — core literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies, preparing students for the Primary School Certificate Examination.

🎒

Junior High School

Grades 7 through 9 — expanded subjects and deeper study, leading to the Junior High School Certificate Examination.

🏛️

Senior High School

Grades 10 through 12 — specialized subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Literature, History, and Economics, preparing students for WASSCE.

📅

Three Terms a Year

Our academic year runs September to June across three terms, with regular assessments and report cards each term.

📝

Continuous Assessment

Class tests, periodic tests, mid-term and final examinations track every student's progress throughout the year.
