Admissions

Join the Excel School System family

We welcome new students to Nursery through Grade 12 at the start of every academic year. Here's how the process works.

The Process

Four Simple Steps

1. Inquiry

Visit our office or contact us to submit an admission inquiry with your child's basic information and the class they're applying for.

2. Review

Our admissions team reviews the inquiry alongside any previous school records and confirms space is available in the requested class.

3. Approval

Once approved, we'll ask you to bring the required documents so we can complete enrollment.

4. Enrollment

Your child is formally enrolled, assigned to their class, and given an admission number — ready to start on day one.

What You'll Need

Documents to Bring

  • Child's birth certificate
  • Previous school report card (if transferring)
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Immunization/health record
  • Parent/guardian identification
  • Emergency contact information
  • Proof of residence
  • Any relevant medical information

Ready to get started?

Reach out to our admissions office and we'll walk you through everything, step by step.
